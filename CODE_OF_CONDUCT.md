# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect respectful, evidence-first collaboration. Be precise, keep disagreements technical, and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or disruptive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of evidence or repository scope.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior undermines a safe and rigorous working environment.
